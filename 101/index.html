A101 - Garden Match: Tiny & Tall

A101 - Garden Match: Tiny & Tall

This game is designed to help children develop their recognition of object size and color. It features two distinct levels:

Level 1: Carrot Match

In this level, players must place carrots into the holes that correspond to their specific size and color.

Level 2: Busy Bees

In the second level, players help bees find their way back to the correct hives by matching them according to color and size.
